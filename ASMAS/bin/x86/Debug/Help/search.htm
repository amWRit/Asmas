You can searh for students or classes based on ID or name. You don't need to know the full name to search. Example: 'sev' will show all results containing the word 'sev'.

You can view details of the student or class. Just right click on any result, and click 'View Details'

When you view details of a class, if you are class teacher of the class, 'Add student' option will be enabled. You can add student to you class.
